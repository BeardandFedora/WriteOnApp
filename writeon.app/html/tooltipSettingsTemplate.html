Available template variables:
  • documentTitle: story title
  • documentMarkdown: story in Markdown format
  • strippedDocumentMarkdown: story without front matter
  • documentHTML: story in HTML format
  • documentHTMLWithFrontMatter
  • frontMatter: undefined if no front matter
  • publishAttributes: undefined if not publishing
Examples:
<title><%= documentTitle %></title>
<div><%- documentHTML %></div>
<%
if(publishAttributes.provider.providerId == "github") print(documentMarkdown);
%>

Careful! Templates are subject to malicious code. Don't copy/paste untrusted content.
